## About Me

I'm a Java engineer with 7 years of experience, primarily working in the power, grid, and energy sector.

Over the years, I've been involved in building enterprise systems that support digital transformation across
energy-related businesses, while also exploring technologies and solutions in the IoT space.

I enjoy building software that is steady, practical, and built for real-world use. My work is rooted in long-term
thinking, engineering discipline, and a belief that good systems should remain reliable under complexity.

- Focused on: Java, Kotlin, Python
- Domain experience: Power grid, energy, IoT
- Blog: [zhaojiaiqing.tech](https://www.zhaojiaiqing.tech)
